Welcome to Public Peer Review Platform

A platform creating a truly open peer review system for the researcher, by the researcher. This is a project initiated by a group doctoral students who believe research experience could be better. In facing the limitations of current peer review system, we propose an alternative preprint system allowing the related community members to review research articles and establish collaboration.
